About

AI strategy, governance, and regulatory expert with 7+ years' experience across government, civil society, consulting, and industry.

I help organisations navigate the complexity of AI, from drafting AI policy to designing operational governance, AI risk assessment frameworks that balance accountability and compliance with innovation at scale.

My experience spans international and multi-stakeholder environments, advising C-suite executives on responsible AI deployment while ensuring alignment with evolving regulatory landscapes.

I also serve as an Honorary Member of the Africa Data Protection Association, supporting strategic relations with African governments and contributing to Data & AI frameworks across the continent.

Approach

I approach AI as a transformative force that must be developed with rigor and humility, embedding safety, evaluation, trustworthiness, and governance at the core so that progress remains societally beneficial.
